AI Ethics & Responsible AI

At AnnualLeaveAI, we are committed to building and deploying artificial intelligence responsibly. Our approach to AI is guided by the following principles:

Transparency

  • We clearly communicate how our AI features work and what data they use.
  • We provide users with information about how decisions are made by our AI systems.

Fairness

  • We strive to ensure our AI is free from bias and treats all users equitably.
  • We regularly review and test our algorithms for fairness and inclusivity.

Privacy

  • We respect user privacy and only use data necessary to provide our services.
  • We never sell or misuse your personal information.

Accountability

  • We take responsibility for the impact of our AI and welcome feedback from users.
  • Contact us at michaelakinbola@hotmail.com with any concerns or questions about our AI ethics.

Continuous Improvement

  • We are committed to ongoing evaluation and improvement of our AI systems as technology and standards evolve.

Learn more about our approach or share your feedback by contacting us at michaelakinbola@hotmail.com.
